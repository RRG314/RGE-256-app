Privacy Policy

RGE-256 is an experimental pseudo-random number generator created for research and educational purposes.

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

  • No user accounts are used.
  • No personal data is collected.
  • No analytics or tracking systems are included.
  • No advertising services are used.
  • No data is sent to external servers.

All computation occurs locally on your device.

If this changes in future versions, this policy will be updated to reflect those changes.
